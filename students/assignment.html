Module 1: Fundamentals of Data Analytics – Knowledge Check

Dr. Emily Thompson

In this module, we will dive into the basics of data analysis, learning how to clean, process, and analyze datasets. Our focus will be on understanding patterns, trends, and insights using jingle industry-standard tools. By the end of this module, you should have a solid understanding of how to explore and validate data before making key decisions.

Instructions

  • Complete each step of the analysis.
  • Reflect on your findings.
  • Validate your insights.
  • Discuss your results.

MCQ Checklist
